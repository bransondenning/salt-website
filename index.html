FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need to be home for the refill?
Nope. Most softeners are in a garage or utility room — we'll arrange access that works for you (garage code, side door, etc.) and text you when we're done.
What kind of salt do you use?
Standard high-purity water softener salt pellets — the same trusted brands you'd buy at the hardware store, without the hauling.
Monthly or every other month?
Your choice. Most homes do monthly. Light salt users can pick every-other-month visits at a per-visit price — same service, half the trips.
What if my tank doesn't need salt this month?
We still check it — and if it's full, your included bags roll over. You never pay for salt you didn't need.
Is there a contract?
No contracts, ever. Pause or cancel anytime with a text.
How do I pay?
Simple billing by card, Venmo, or cash — set up once on your first visit, then it's automatic.
